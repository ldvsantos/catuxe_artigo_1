\noindent
\textbf{Manuscript:} ``Machine Learning Readiness to Support Agroecological Transitions in Traditional Agricultural Systems: A Meta-Analysis''

\vspace{12pt}

\noindent
This study is a meta-analysis and scoping review based exclusively on previously published, publicly available literature. No primary data collection involving human participants, animal subjects, or biological samples was conducted. Accordingly, no ethical approval or informed consent was required. All data sources are fully cited and the complete bibliographic corpus is deposited in a public repository (Open Science Framework, DOI: 10.17605/OSF.IO/J7STC).
